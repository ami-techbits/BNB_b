BNB Alumni Network

Once BNB, always BNB — a community of changemakers connected across the globe.

The BNB Alumni Network honors the achievements of our graduates and fosters lifelong connection between alumni, current students, and the school community. From college acceptances and career milestones to community impact and global leadership, our alumni continue to shape the world in meaningful ways.

We believe graduation is not goodbye — it’s the beginning of a new chapter with BNB. Whether you're nearby or across the world, you're always part of the family.

🎉 Alumni Events & Traditions

BNB alumni continue to connect, give back, and celebrate their journey through special events that keep the spirit of our school alive.

🎓 Alumni Reunion Weekend

Graduates from around the world return to reconnect with classmates and staff. Stories, laughter, and legacy are shared every year.

📢 Alumni Guest Lectures

Distinguished alumni return to speak to current students, sharing real-world experience in business, science, health, and the arts.

💼 Career Day

A tradition where alumni mentor students through workshops, Q&As, and panels — building bridges between ambition and reality.

🤝 Alumni Day of Service

Giving back is part of who we are. Alumni team up with students for school improvement projects and community outreach.
