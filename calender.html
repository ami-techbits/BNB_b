Academic Calendar

Staying in sync with your child’s learning journey — every term, every milestone.

Our academic calendar reflects the rhythm of meaningful learning — from term start dates and holidays to exams and school-wide events. Designed to support families in planning ahead, the calendar ensures clear communication and shared expectations throughout the year.

Whether you're preparing for parent-teacher conferences, cultural celebrations, or student showcases, this schedule helps keep you connected to the pulse of school life. With a balance of academic focus and community engagement, every date is a step in your child’s growth story.
