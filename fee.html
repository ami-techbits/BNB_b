School Fees

Transparent, fair, and structured to support quality education at every level.

At BNB Learning Center, our fee structure is designed to reflect the value of the educational experience we provide — balancing affordability with academic excellence, modern facilities, and strong student support systems.

We offer clear breakdowns by level (KG, Elementary, High School), along with available payment plans and policies on registration, uniforms, and textbooks. Our finance office is ready to assist families with any questions and provide flexibility where possible.

💼 School Fee Guidelines & Payment Structure

A complete breakdown of what you’re paying, why it matters, and how to manage it smoothly throughout the year.

💳 Fee Structure by Level

Fees are categorized by academic level (KG, Elementary, and High School). Each level includes tuition, technology, and student activity fees. Textbooks, uniforms, and transportation are billed separately.

📅 Payment Schedule

Payments are accepted annually, per semester, or monthly (by arrangement). Due dates are clearly communicated before each term begins. Prompt payments ensure uninterrupted services and academic access.

🏷️ Registration & Admission Fees

A one-time registration fee is required for new students. This covers administrative processing, entrance assessments, and onboarding materials.

🧾 What’s Included

Core tuition includes instructional materials, student portals, lab resources (Grades 4+), and participation in school events and assemblies.

🚫 What’s Not Included

Fees for school uniforms, transportation, international exams (SAT, IGCSE, etc.), private tutoring, and extracurricular trips are billed separately.

💸 Refund & Withdrawal Policy

Registration fees are non-refundable. Tuition refunds for early withdrawals are prorated based on the school’s refund policy. No refunds after midterm.

⚠️ Late Payment Policy

Late payments incur a small administrative fee. Students may not receive report cards or exam access until accounts are settled.

🤝 Financial Support & Discounts

Scholarships, sibling discounts, and financial hardship plans are available upon request. Please consult the admissions or finance office.
