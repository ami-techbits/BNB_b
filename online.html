Online Application

Start your child’s journey with BNB from anywhere, anytime.

At BNB Learning Center, we've made applying simple, accessible, and stress-free through our online application portal. Whether you're enrolling in Kindergarten, Elementary, or High School, our process ensures clarity, security, and personalized communication every step of the way.

The application includes basic student information, document upload (like transcripts or birth certificates), and an opportunity to share more about your child's strengths and goals. Our admissions team is always available to support you.

Download Form

📋 Online Application Requirements

To ensure a smooth and complete application process, please prepare the following documents and information before submitting your form online.

🧒 Student Information

Full legal name, gender, date of birth, nationality, and passport-size photo. For returning students, provide your Student ID (if known).

📄 Academic Records

Upload the latest official school report card or transcript. Transfer students should include reports from the last two academic years.

📑 Identification Documents

A scanned copy of the student’s birth certificate and passport (or national ID). Foreign applicants should also include a valid visa or residency permit.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parent/Guardian Info

Full names of parents/guardians, contact numbers, email addresses, physical address, and occupation details.

📝 Personal Statement (Grades 6+)

A short written statement from the student explaining their goals, interests, and why they want to join BNB Learning Center.

💉 Medical Clearance

A copy of the student’s recent medical check-up or immunization record. Notify the school of any known medical conditions or allergies.

💳 Application Fee Receipt

Proof of payment for the non-refundable application fee. Bank details and payment options are provided on the application page.
